Editor's Note

Artificial Intelligence is no longer just a concept from science fiction. Today, it is becoming part of our classrooms, helping students learn in new and exciting ways. From automated grading systems to intelligent tutoring programs, AI is transforming the way education works.

As students in the digital age, it is important for us to understand how technology influences learning. This issue explores the benefits, challenges, and future possibilities of AI in education.

Author's Insight

From my perspective, I believe that Artificial Intelligence can become a powerful partner in education if used responsibly. It can help teachers save time and give students personalized learning experiences.
